Tough Times forged from fires of a global pandemic

To fulfill the purpose stated above, we are building the most unique app of the post-COVID world, which is not an app, but a discipline.

The seed idea of this discipline took birth from lessons learned in the year 2020. These lessons could not have been learned any other way had we not lived through this incredible period of history.

This was the first time a global pandemic happened since the dawn of digital internet era, that we all live in now. And like every other era of human civilization, we are faced with new kinds of challenges.

Personal Network Discipline is an effort that was born from living some such challenges. Wisdom from various sources is driving the creation of this unique app, which is not an app, but a discipline.

What you are reading now are some of our early thoughts on how the *Personal Network Yoga Discipline* app should work and how it should not.

We respectfully disagree, but we also appreciate this rather interesting outlook on wealth.

As a team of artsy entrepreneurs, we are committed to learning from wisdom all around our planet, but our primary focus is to build real solutions of our times, for real problems of our times.

So we have borrowed from that knowledge, of different kinds of wealth, and have both modified it and added to it. Allow us to share with you how we now classify wealth to be of 9 different kinds, and how we think you shouldn't treat them equally, but instead assign different priorities to them, that you should change based on time and context and your current phase in life.
